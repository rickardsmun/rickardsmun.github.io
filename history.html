Model United Nations is an extra-curricular activity where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues. These activities take place at MUN Conferences, which are usually organized by a College MUN Club. Once a school has registered for a conference, it receives a country to represent. Each student on that team will represent that country in a different committee with different topics. After assigned your Country, Committee, and Tpoics, you will have to look into your Background Guides, and use other resources to peapare for your conference. You will need to prepare a Position Paper, Opening Speech, and a Research Binder. Thousands of middle, high, and college students across the world participate in the Model United Nations Conferences, which involve researching, public speaking, debating, and writing skills, along with critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership abilities.
Learn more about Model United Nations at the Official United Nations Webpage.
